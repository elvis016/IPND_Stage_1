Lesson 4.1: Networks

A network is a group of entities that can communicate, even though they are not all directly connected.

  • Latency: Time it takes meassage to get from source to destination
  • Bandwidth: Amount of information that can be transmitted per unit time
  • Protocols: Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP)
